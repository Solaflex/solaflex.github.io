Quarantine

G2 Hotel in Seoul

Seoul has seen a steady increase of Corona cases during the summer 2021. My initial hope to get a Quarantine Excemption Certificate was vanished by the time I applied for my visa. The South Korean government was not willing to reduce the rules in favor of foreigners while the Corona cases were increasing. This would have been a suicide mission for every politician. Therefore I had to sit out my 14 nights of mandatory quarantine in the G2 hotel. The food was ok-ish but not enough. It was therefore also a short diet for myself :)
